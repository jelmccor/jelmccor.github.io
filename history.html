 History
Patricia McCormick is the First American Woman to fight bulls in Mexico.
  • First Bullfight (Sept. 1951)
  • Matador's Union (Jan. 1952)
  • First Professional Fight (Jan. 1952)
  • Total Corridas Fought: 300
  • Top Billing During 10 Year Career
   
Patricia passed away on March 26, 2013 in Del Rio, Texas.
